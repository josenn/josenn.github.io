Level Designer

I am passionate about games and game design. In particular Level Design. Level Design is where all facets of the creation process come together to form a cohesive experience that a player will enjoy. It brings together programming, art, sound, writing and everything else in between. I love the communication that comes with it. Listening to new ideas, talking to (and making friends with) amazing people is one of the most exciting parts of game development for me. Beyond games, in my free time, I enjoy watching anime and spending time with my partner. I'm also a tech nerd in general so outside of normal game development I do some programming as a hobby.
